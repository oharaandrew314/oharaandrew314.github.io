TrustGrapher Open Source

Simulation Tool built during my internship at Carleton University to assist a graduate student with his reasearch on Peer Trust Algorithms. Events are passed in via file, and played through, displaying a graphical representation of the current state. Events can be dynamically injected, and the simulation time and playback speed can be altered at runtime.
